About Dry Oar Rafting

Our Mission

At Dry Oar Rafting, our mission is to provide safe, thrilling, and memorable whitewater rafting adventures for people of all experience levels.

Our History

Founded in 2002, Dry Oar Rafting has guided thousands of adventurers through the most scenic river canyons in the region. Our expert team has over 20 years of combined experience.
